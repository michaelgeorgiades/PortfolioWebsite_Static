# Privacy Policy for Convertinator

**Last Updated: December 7, 2025**

## Overview

Convertinator ("the App") is a unit conversion application. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

## Information We Collect

**We do not collect any personal information.**

The App does not:
- Require user registration or login
- Collect names, email addresses, or contact information
- Track your location
- Access your contacts, photos, or files
- Use analytics or tracking services
- Store your conversion history
- Use advertising or ad tracking

## Network Usage

The App makes network requests solely to fetch current currency exchange rates from a third-party API (exchangerate-api.com). This request:
- Does not include any personal or device information
- Is used only to display up-to-date currency conversion rates
- Falls back to cached rates if the network is unavailable

We do not control the privacy practices of third-party services. You can review the privacy policy of exchangerate-api.com on their website.

## Data Storage

All data entered into the App (such as values for conversion) is processed locally on your device and is not transmitted to any server. No conversion data is stored persistently.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
